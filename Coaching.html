The Nuances

What does QHC 5D technology consist of? expand_more
It is a form of accompaniment that unites soul wisdom with energy science. '5D' or Fifth Dimension is a state of consciousness where we remember that we are not limited by fear or the past. Through this loving technology, we work on your vibrational field to release blockages and allow your body and mind to tune into a frequency of peace, health, and conscious creation.
Do I need prior experience in meditation or quantum physics? expand_more
All you need is the genuine desire to meet yourself. My job is to guide you step by step, in a simple and contained way. Your body and your energy already know how to heal; I only help you open the door and remember that path.
How does a Quantum Healing session feel? expand_more
Each experience is unique, but most people describe a feeling of deep relaxation, mental clarity, and expansion. It's like a deep rest where your nervous system regulates and your energy aligns.
What is the difference between a Yoga session and a QHC session? expand_more
Yoga helps us inhabit the body and calm the mind through movement and breath. A QHC session is a deeper work on your 'energetic biography,' where we look for the origin of certain patterns to transform them from the root. Both practices nurture each other for your complete well-being.
How many sessions do I need to see results? expand_more
In the path of the Quantum Healer Coach, we understand that every soul has its own rhythm and every process is unique. Therefore, we do not work with a fixed number of sessions, but through an accompaniment plan designed specifically for you, adapted to what your being needs to integrate at this moment.
