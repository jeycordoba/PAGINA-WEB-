Your Instructor

Meet Your Guide,
Jennifer Cordoba

"Yoga is not about touching your toes, it's about what you learn on the way down."

With over a decade of dedicated practice and teaching, my journey began in the high-stress world of corporate design. Searching for balance, I found my way onto a yoga mat and never looked back.

My approach combines the fluid movements of Vinyasa with the grounding principles of Hatha, always emphasizing the connection between breath and movement. I believe that every body is unique, and my classes are designed to be inclusive, challenging, and deeply restorative.

When I'm not in the studio, you'll likely find me exploring local hiking trails or practicing meditation in my garden. I am a lifelong student of movement and mindfulness.
