Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership? expand_more
Our Monthly Unlimited plan requires a 30-day notice for cancellation. Annual memberships are non-refundable but can be transferred to another person if you move.
Do you offer student discounts? expand_more
Yes, we offer a 15% discount for full-time students and seniors (65+). Please bring a valid ID to the studio to have the discount applied.
Can I pause my membership? expand_more
Monthly Unlimited and Annual members can pause their membership for up to 30 days once per year at no additional cost.
What should I bring? expand_more
Comfortable clothing is key. We recommend bringing a water bottle and your own mat, though we have high-quality mats available for rent.
