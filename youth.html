Tailored Youth Programs

Ages 5-11

Infinite Kids

A playful introduction to yoga and emotional regulation. We use storytelling, music, and creative movement to help children find their inner calm while building physical strength and coordination.

In these sessions, your child will learn how to identify their emotions and use their breath as a tool for grounding. It's more than just physical movement—it's about building a foundation of self-awareness that they will carry through their entire development.

  • check_circle Mindful storytelling and creative play
  • check_circle Emotional intelligence exercises
  • check_circle Foundation for lifelong focused concentration
Ages 12-18

Infinite Teens

Navigating the complexities of adolescence requires more than academic preparation. Our teen program focuses on stress management, physical resilience, and building a secure sense of self.

Through dynamic vinyasa flows and targeted mindfulness sessions, we help teenagers manage exam anxiety, social pressures, and digital overwhelm. They leave with a toolkit of practical techniques to maintain balance in a high-speed world.

  • check_circle Advanced stress-reduction techniques
  • check_circle Body positivity and core physical strength
  • check_circle Resilience training for academic and social challenges

Common Questions

What ages do you work with? expand_more
We have specialized programs for two distinct groups: Infinite Kids (ages 6–12) and Infinite Teens (ages 13–18). Each approach is developmentally tailored to their emotional and physical needs.
Do they need prior experience? expand_more
Not at all. Our programs are designed to be inclusive and accessible to everyone, meeting every student exactly where they are.
Are instructors certified? expand_more
Yes, all instructors hold current background checks and are certified in child-specific mindfulness and yoga instruction.
What is the duration of the sessions? expand_more
Standard youth sessions are 60 minutes, focusing on a balance of physical movement and guided mindfulness.
Are parents allowed to stay? expand_more
For Infinite Kids, parents are welcome to observe from the sanctuary lounge. For Infinite Teens, we encourage an independent practice to build self-reliance.
